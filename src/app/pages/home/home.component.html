What is "virtrolio"?

Virtrolio stands for ‘virtual portfolio’ and is an online version of those pages in your yearbook that you use to write personal and long-lasting messages to your friends. In other words, you can create your own virtrolio, share it with friends, and have them write customized messages for you to keep. Whenever you want, you can view your virtrolio or download it for keepsakes.
