What is virtrolio?

Virtrolio is your virtual folio - an online version of those last few pages in your yearbook that you use to write personal and long-lasting messages to your friends. In other words, you can create your own virtrolio, share it with friends, and have them write customized messages for you to keep. Whenever you want, you can view your virtrolio and all the messages you have received from your friends!

Why use virtrolio?

Virtrolio offers you an authentic yearbook signing experience, and that means privacy. You can only view virtrolio messages sent directly to you (see our Privacy Policy for more details).

How can I make a virtrolio?

It's easy to create a virtrolio! Sign in with your Google account and a custom virtrolio link will be generated for you. Share this link with your friends so they can sign your virtrolio.
