Can't picture your perfect message?

In order for us to display your image, we need something called an embed link. This embed link isn't always easy to come by, depending on where you're trying to get the image from.

Not to worry, though! In a couple minutes you can follow the steps below to get the 'embed link' for a photo stored on Google Photos!

  1. Upload your photo on Google Photos.
  2. Get the 'share link' for your Google photo (click the share button when viewing the photo).
    Note: this 'share' link is not the same as the required 'embed link'.
  3. Go to this website and convert your 'sharing' link into an 'embed' link.
  4. The embed link should now work in the preview! Try it out!

Unfortunately, for now you'll have to get these 'embed links' yourself. We will be implementing ways to more generate them from Google Drive / Google Photos / other platforms using the sharing links which these services usually provide to users more easily.
