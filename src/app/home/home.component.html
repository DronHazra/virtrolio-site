What is "virtrolio"?

Virtrolio stands for ‘virtual portfolio’ and is an online version of those pages in your yearbook that you use to write messages to your friends. You can create your own virtrolio, share it with friends, and have them write messages on it. Whenever you want, you can view your virtrolio or download it.

Tentative release: Mid-summer 2020.

Stay tuned and contact us at virtrolio.team@gmail.com if you have any questions.
